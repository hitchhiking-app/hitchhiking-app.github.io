We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal information: full name, birthday, email, password.
  • Ride details: date, time, route.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To enable ride matching and communication between users.
  • To improve and secure the App.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Contract necessity (providing the service).
  • User consent (when required, e.g., marketing).
  • Legal obligations (accounting, security).

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with anybody.
  • Data may be disclosed if required by law.

5. Data Retention

  • We keep your data as long as you have an account.
  • You may request deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • access your data;
  • request correction or deletion;
  • withdraw consent;
  • object to processing;
  • file a complaint with a supervisory authority.

7. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

8. Contact

For questions about privacy, contact us at: team@hitchhiking.app
